Why is Zooted is The Best Linktree Alternative

January 29th, 2019 · 4 min read

If you’ve ever used Instagram and looking for linktree alternative, you’ll know that it’s an incredible tool for getting people to fall in love with your business! However, if you ever want to convert your 10,000+ followers into actual paying clients or customers, you’ll need a way to direct them to your website or to your blog, which is where the actual buying and booking happens.

There’s only one minor issue: Insta isn’t known for being generous with clickable links as a forum. You will only receive one connection. The all-powerful “link in bio.” That’s your one chance to bring followers from your social media to the exact location they need to be on your website.

You should now guide the link in your bio to your home page, where they will (hopefully) find the link you described in your Instagram Stories for themselves… However, because people have been conditioned to expect immediate gratification and ease, they are more likely to search for something for precisely 0.02 seconds before giving up and returning to mindlessly scrolling their feed.

LINKTREE INTRODUCTION

(A third-party app that appears to be able to fix all of your Instagram bio connection issues.)

What exactly is Linktree?

Consider Linktree as a landing page for all of the places you’ve connected in your bio recently. You don’t have to choose which link to prioritize, and it can also be used to direct people to the pages you think are most interesting for them to see if they’re visiting your site for the first time.

Linktree has a number of useful features. (And at just $6 a month, it’s also unlikely to break the bank.) However, there is a better, much less expensive choice to consider!

Why is Zooted the best Linktree alternative?

IT’S COMPLETELY FREE

To some extent, indeed. Why not take advantage of the fact that you’re already paying for your site and use it to create your own connection in a bio landing page?

You’ve worked hard for it! Instead, put that $6 a month toward your posh Starbs order.

LINKTREE IS NOT AS CUSTOMIZABLE AS ZOOTED.

There’s no need to spend time learning how to replicate your site’s vibe in a completely different (and, let’s be frank, pretty limited) medium if you’ve already built an entire site.

In Zooted, you can build your own landing page for your connection in bio!

You can keep your connection in the bio page looking completely on-brand by using the site designs, fonts, and colors you’ve already spent hours setting up. If you want to get very fancy (more about why that may not be a good idea later in this post), you can use some of Linktree’s integrations or content types (like newsletter sign-ups and video) with a lot more flexibility in terms of layout, spacing, and so on.

ZOOTED IS BETTER FOR SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMIZATION

There’s a lot that goes into ranking #1 in a Google search for keywords in your industry, but there are two main determining factors:

  • 1. How long do visitors usually stay on your site?
  • 2. The number of sites they look at when they’re there.

The internet’s tiny little robots are pretty smart, but since they can’t read, they depend on internet users’ actions to decide how important your content is, and therefore how likely you are to have the answers to any given quest.

Rather than give all those clicks to some third-party applications like Linktree, you can boost your site’s SEO by hosting your link in bio page on your own website, which will result in more page visits and overall time spent surfing.

ALL OF YOUR SITE ANALYTICS CAN BE KEPT IN ONE LOCATION.

It’s great that Linktree thought to incorporate analytics in their operation, but it’s another thing you’ll have to log into at the end of the week to monitor your site metrics.

Most likely, you’ve already set up your platform to track metrics with Zooted or Google analytics, and having all of your website’s analytics in one location makes it more likely to be tracked.

Why should I keep track of my stats link?

You need a way to determine how well your current custom landing page is doing because if you don’t monitor those metrics consistently, you won’t have anything to compare it to.

If social media is your primary marketing tool, you’ll want to keep track of how many clicks each connection on your link in the bio landing page receives each week in a spreadsheet.

If a link isn’t getting a lot of clicks, it’s probably not really interesting to your audience and shouldn’t be taking up space on your link in the bio tab.

You also don’t have to worry about losing access to past data if you cancel your Linktree subscription down the road if you DIY your landing page in Zooted.

TO MANAGE ALL YOUR SITE TRAFFIC, YOU ONLY NEED ONE PLATFORM.

Things break from time to time, and if you’re not in there changing your links every day, it may be several hours or even days before you know.

Meanwhile, your Instagram followers are annoyed and perplexed because the connection you promised isn’t working. They won’t be able to just try a different link to get to your site because you’re only allowed one link on your profile, and the chances of them following through with a link click in the future are slim.

It’s one less place to log in to get the job done, and you can upgrade or patch a connection on the fly by logging into your account.
